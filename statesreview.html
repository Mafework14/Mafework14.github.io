Review Of States

My Rankings:

My Top Choice:

Nebraska

I enjoyed Nebraska's website because they had the most pleasing colors out of the other four websites I looked at. The website is not overly flashy. I had no problems finding any information, the website was quick to load and very easy to navigate. The background of the front page was a beautiful picture of the city. The website also displayed a colorful graph with solid information.

My Second Choice:

Illinois

Illinois is my second favorite website that I viewed.They displayed the most creative website. In the middle of the page they have a “Illinois Virtual Assistant.” I used it and it was very helpful guiding me through the website with ease. This website also has a lot of helpful information about the state and what they are about.

My Third Choice:

Florida

Florida was my third favorite website. Their homepage is an exquisite picture of a sunset. They have a search bar where someone can ask whatever they want. However the page did not display a lot of information. Besides the sunset picture, the website was simple.

My Fourth Choice:

Alaska

Alaska had a ton of information on their website. They had different dropdowns and links. The website was somewhat overwhelming. The colors were not very eye-pleasing and there was not a ton of pictures.

My Last Choice:

Delaware

Delaware was my least favorite website out of the five I had the chance to analyze. I feel like this website had too much information about covid-19. The website's first two pages are all information about covid with alerts and cautions. The website's colors were the least eye pleasing.
